Introduction

A generalist profile shaped by business, design and technology.

I started from a business and financial background, working with product teams, commercial areas and operational journeys in fintech and banking environments. Over time, I became increasingly interested in how digital products are experienced by people. Not only how they perform as business assets.

Today, my work sits at the intersection of Product Strategy, Digital Experience Design and Business Analysis. I use journey mapping, funnel analysis, research and stakeholder collaboration to identify friction points, improve decision-making and design experiences that are clearer, faster and more useful.
