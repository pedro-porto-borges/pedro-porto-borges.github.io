Project Three

Wise Quests: Turning Financial Freedom into a Guided Learning Journey

A product concept for Wise Young Explorer that helps parents give teenagers more financial autonomy through visibility, challenges and goal-based saving — balancing parental reassurance with teen motivation.

Overview

Wise Young Explorer helps teenagers save, spend and receive money across currencies while parents retain oversight and control. The challenge was to design an experience that feels reassuring for parents but still simple, useful and motivating for teens.

The key tension was clear: parents want safety and responsibility, while teenagers want freedom and independence. The solution needed to work for both audiences without directly marketing to minors or creating a separate brand.

Process

My contribution focused on research, interviews, insight synthesis, ideation, wireframes and case narrative. Together with the team, we structured the solution around three pillars: Visibility, Challenges and Goals.

Visibility gives teens access to basic money awareness through balance, notifications and spending tracking. Challenges turn everyday responsibilities into rewarded actions. Goals help teens connect short-term financial behavior with future aspirations.
