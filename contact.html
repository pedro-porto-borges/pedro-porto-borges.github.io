Short bio

I'm a Product Designer based in Lisbon with a background that most designers don't have — four years in financial services, an accounting degree, and hands-on experience optimizing journeys for millions of users in retail banking.

I design digital products by connecting three things: what users need, what the business requires, and what the data reveals. My work spans journey mapping, conversion funnel analysis, UX research, and product strategy — always focused on creating measurable impact.

Currently completing a postgraduate in Digital Experience Design at the University of Lisbon, I'm exploring Product Design and Product Management opportunities where strategic thinking, business acumen, and user-centered design come together.
